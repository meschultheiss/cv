Portfolio

Patents in Switzerland nlp

Collected for over 50 years of patents abstracts authenticated in Switzerland. Computed topic extraction (LDA) and dimension reduction (Fisher-LDA) to visualize the semantic path of innovation in Switzerland through the last 50 years: from chemistry to information sciences.

Error detection in archives nlp

The goal of the project was to detect and correct errors introduced in the Le Temps digital archive (~4M news articles published over the last 2 centuries). Leveraged a full NLP pipeline to detect errors with 93% precision, enabling to spot outlying years with particularly high error rates and giving insights about OCR error types.

Wine Price Forecasting data-viz

Scraped weather, price and grade data for all french wine regions. Built an interactive dashboard to give data-driven back-ups on weather, wine quality and wine prices correlations. Also explored regression models to forecast the first retail price of a wine given weather and its past grades.

Image Classification computer vision

As a first project in Machine Learning we were given pictures of 4 classes from ImageNet and our task was to classify them using usual ML classifiers. Best score was achieved with CNN features.
